    What I'm building & why

    Hyderabad's official maps don't match the ground. Water bodies are encroached, roads are unclassified, slums are invisible. This project exists to close that gap.

    It's also a learning project. PostGIS, MapLibre, satellite NDWI — all new territory. The map is the excuse; the skills are the point.

    If the data gets good enough, it may be useful to urban planners, journalists, and residents. But that's not the constraint for v1.
